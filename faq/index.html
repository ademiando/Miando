Frequently Asked Questions

What is Montamap?
Montamap is an outdoor adventure platform connecting explorers with curated hiking routes, local guides, and emergency support tools.
Is Montamap free to use?
Yes, Montamap offers free access to basic features. Premium packages are available for additional tools, offline maps, and advanced safety features.
Do I need to share my location?
Only when you activate GPS-based features like SOS or trail navigation. You can always control this in your app settings.
Can I book local guides through Montamap?
Yes! You can explore, compare, and book verified local guides directly through our platform.
How secure is my data?
We use encrypted communication and secure infrastructure. Your personal data is never sold, and we comply with global privacy standards.
What devices support Montamap?
Montamap works on most modern browsers and is available on both Android and iOS.
