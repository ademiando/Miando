Trekking Routes

Pura Besakih Route: Longest and most scenic route. Approx. 7–8 hours to summit. Starts from west and offers sunrise view.

Pura Pasar Agung Route: Shorter but steeper route. Approx. 4–5 hours to crater ridge, not summit.

Junggul / Redung Route: Lesser-known eastern routes. Usually requires guide support. Steep terrain and remote access.

About Mount Agung

Mount Agung is the highest peak in Bali and considered a sacred mountain by the Balinese. It dominates the surrounding landscape and offers one of the most rewarding sunrise treks in Indonesia.

Due to its volcanic activity, hikers must check the latest updates and follow safety protocols. The mountain provides dramatic views of Lombok and Rinjani on clear days.
