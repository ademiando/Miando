About Prau

Gunung Prau offers one of the best sunrise views in Indonesia, showcasing the silhouettes of Sindoro, Sumbing, Merapi, and Merbabu. Located in the heart of Central Java, this mountain is a favorite for beginner to intermediate hikers. The summit is a wide grassy hilltop, making it a great camping destination.

The trek is short, but the reward is immense—cloud carpet mornings, golden hour vistas, and cool alpine air. Popular among local hikers and nature photographers, Prau is accessible almost year-round with proper gear.
