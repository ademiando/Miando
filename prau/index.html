Trekking Routes

Patak Banteng: Popular and short route with direct access to the summit ridgeline. Great for sunrise views.

Dwarawati: Offers a forested trail with moderate incline. Less crowded than Patak Banteng.

Igirmranak: Scenic route with peaceful atmosphere. Slightly longer, suitable for campers.

Kalilembu: Moderate difficulty, beautiful pine forest trail, connects with Dieng route.

Via Dieng: Longer route with panoramic views of Dieng Plateau. Ideal for nature lovers.

About Prau

Mount Prau is a favorite hiking destination in Central Java, famous for its wide summit ridge that offers stunning views of other peaks like Sindoro, Sumbing, and Merbabu. It’s known for being beginner-friendly with relatively short trails.

Camping at the summit is popular to witness the “golden sunrise,” especially during dry season. The mountain is managed by several local basecamps and communities who help maintain the environment and guide hikers safely.
