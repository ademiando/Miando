About Prau

Mount Prau is known for its breathtaking sunrise above the clouds and panoramic views of surrounding peaks like Sumbing, Sindoro, and Merapi. Ideal for beginner hikers and weekend adventures, Prau is a popular choice for local and international trekkers.

Located in the Dieng Plateau, it's a biodiverse highland ecosystem filled with volcanic landscapes, geothermal fields, and Javanese culture.
