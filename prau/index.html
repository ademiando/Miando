Trekking Routes

Patak Banteng: Most popular route. Short and steep. Suitable for sunrise hikers.

Dieng: Longer but more gradual. Offers scenic views along the way.

Kalilembu: Less crowded, moderate difficulty, good for beginners.

Igirmranak: Forested path with fewer crowds. Natural atmosphere.

Wates: Newer route with gradual ascent. Peaceful and clean.

About Prau

Mount Prau is known for its spectacular sunrise views, golden hills, and rolling grassy peaks. It's one of the most accessible mountains in Java for beginner hikers and a favorite for weekend trekking.

Its summit provides panoramic views of nearby volcanoes such as Sumbing, Sindoro, and Merbabu. The route is short but rewarding, making it a perfect introduction to hiking in Indonesia.
